03 | ReadOnly
I've been reading Hindi to gain exposure and learn new vocabulary words. Whenever I came across an unfamiliar word in Hindi I would highlight it, go onto the Shabdkosh Hindi to English dictionary tab, enter the word, get the translation and gender and then add the information to my online flashcards. When you know as little Hindi as I do this takes time! So, I made a program where a user inputs Hindi text and can highlight individual words. When an individual word is highlighted, an option comes up to translate, romanize, and add to spreadsheet. When I click add to spreadsheet, the word in Hindi and its English translation is added to an Excel spreadsheet. I can add the spreadsheet information to flashcards later and stay immersed in the story!

Tools used: Ajax, Javascript
